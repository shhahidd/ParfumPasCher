Azure Mist

Eau de Parfum Intense

Azure Mist embodies the concept of slow excellence through its use of rare coastal botanicals. It is a crisp, revitalizing fragrance that mirrors the cooling sensation of mist on sun drenched marble. Light yet enduring, it carries a refined mineral quality that feels both aquatic and sophisticated, perfect for daytime elegance.

Olfactory Notes

  • Top: Sea Salt, Bergamot Zest, Neroli
  • Heart: White Cyclamen, Crushed Mint, Ozone
  • Base: Driftwood, White Musk, Ambrette Seed
