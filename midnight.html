Midnight Saffron

Eau de Parfum Intense

Midnight Saffron is a study in tension and mystery. It opens with the piercing brilliance of hand-harvested Persian saffron, grounded by the smoky, leather-like depth of black oud. Designed for those who command the room without speaking, this scent lingers like a shared secret, unfolding in layers of warmth and dark intensity.

The Notes

  • Top: Dark Plum, Pink Pepper, Rum
  • Heart: Black Orchid, Jasmine Sambac, Ylang-Ylang
  • Base: Vanilla Bean, Sandalwood, Indonesian Patchouli
